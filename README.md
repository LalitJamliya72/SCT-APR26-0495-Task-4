# 📊 Marketing Campaign EDA - Business Insights Report

## 🔍 Project Overview

This project performs **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)** on a marketing campaign dataset to understand campaign performance and generate business insights.
The focus is on analyzing **ROI (Return on Investment)**, **conversion rates**, and **marketing funnel performance** instead of building machine learning models.

---

## 🎯 Objective

* Analyze marketing campaign data
* Identify high-performing channels
* Understand customer behavior through funnel analysis
* Recommend budget allocation based on ROI

---

## 📁 Dataset

* Source: Kaggle
* Dataset: Marketing Campaign Dataset
* Features include:

  * Channel (Google, Facebook, Email, etc.)
  * Impressions
  * Clicks
  * Conversions
  * Cost
  * Revenue

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* Python 🐍
* Pandas
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

---

## 🔄 Steps Performed

### 1. Data Cleaning

* Removed missing values
* Dropped duplicate records

### 2. Feature Engineering

* Calculated **ROI**
* Calculated **Conversion Rate**

### 3. Exploratory Data Analysis

* Revenue by Channel
* ROI by Channel
* Funnel Analysis (Impressions → Clicks → Conversions)

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Some channels generate high revenue but low ROI
* Certain channels provide better conversion rates at lower cost
* Significant drop observed in the conversion funnel

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Increase budget for high ROI channels
* Reduce spending on underperforming campaigns
* Optimize landing pages to improve conversions

---

## 📌 Conclusion

EDA helps in understanding the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and enables data-driven decision-making for maximizing profitability.
